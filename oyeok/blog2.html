Gaming and Real Estate. Doesn't sound like a match right? Gaming has for the longest time been the forefront of technology when it comes to consumer electronics because you have various new technologies coming up such as #Playstation4, #PSVR, #XboxOneS all these new things coming up and diving into markets where you wouldn't believe possible. For e.g VR which is an abbreviation for Virtual Reality, This technology is used to help consumers in realistic virtual realities that is brought over by high tech headsets to help consumers experience the setting as if they were there. These VR headsets are used by some builders working on large projects so that investors can see what the end product will be like. Like that OyeOk has made good use of gaming to make your experience of using the app a fun one. The game places you n the shoes of an investor where you have to buy and sell real estate which is based on real time prices of the real properties which you will be buying and selling. This makes for an intuitive experience for app users where they can develop strategies and challenge themselves to gather high income form the right kind of investment. OyeOk has made this game so users can get a gist of what it feels like to invest in property and wait for the right time to buy and sell properties to make a profit. Also the game can be used by app users when travelling incase they are bored and wish to let loose a little but they can use the app to let off a little bit of steam by playing the game.
